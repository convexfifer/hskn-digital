Privacy Policy

Introduction

HSKN Digital ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

Information We Collect

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our apps, including app interactions and feature usage.

Purchase Information: When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide and maintain our services
• Improve and personalize your experience
• Process transactions
• Send important updates about our services

Data Storage

Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload personal data to external servers unless explicitly required for service functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for analytics and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our educational apps, including Prophet Stories, are designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Contact Us
